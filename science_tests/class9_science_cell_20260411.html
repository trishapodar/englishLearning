Q7. [2 Marks]

Answer: Diffusion is the movement of substances from higher to lower concentration. Osmosis is the passage of water from a region of high water concentration through a semi-permeable membrane to a region of low water concentration.

Marking: Correct definition or point of difference for diffusion (1 mark) + for osmosis (1 mark).

Accept: Osmosis requires a semi-permeable membrane; diffusion does not.

Common Error: Confusing the concentration gradient of solute vs solvent.

Ref: NCERT Class 9 Science, Ch. 5

Q14. [3 Marks]

Answer: Prokaryotic: Small size, undefined nuclear region (nucleoid) without nuclear membrane, single chromosome, lack membrane-bound organelles. Eukaryotic: Larger size, well-defined nucleus with nuclear membrane, multiple chromosomes, contain membrane-bound organelles.

Marking: Any three correct contrasting points (1 mark each).

Accept: Valid tabular differences.

Common Error: Confusing prokaryotic with plant cell characteristics.

Ref: NCERT Class 9 Science, Ch. 5

Q15. [5 Marks]

Answer: (a) The nucleus has a double-layered covering called the nuclear membrane, containing pores to allow transfer of material. Inside, it contains chromosomes (DNA and proteins) which are visible as rod-shaped structures when the cell is about to divide, or as chromatin material otherwise. (b) It plays a central role in cellular reproduction (cell division) and acts as the control center of the cell, directing chemical activities and determining the cell's development and form.

Marking: Structure of nucleus including membrane, pores, and chromosomes/chromatin (3 marks) + Role in reproduction (1 mark) + Role in controlling activities (1 mark).

Accept: Mention of nucleolus or genes.

Common Error: Missing the detail about chromatin vs chromosomes.

Ref: NCERT Class 9 Science, Ch. 5
