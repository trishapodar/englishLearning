Q8.   Pictograph Creation   [3 Marks]

→ Apples (20 / 5) = 4 circles. (O O O O)

Marking: 1 mark for correctly drawing 4 circles for Apples. 1 mark for 7 circles for Oranges. 1 mark for 3 circles for Bananas.

Ref: NCERT Class 4 Mathematics, Ch. 12

Q9.   Chapati Chart Fractions   [3 Marks]

→ Total = 40 students.

→ (i) Cricket = \(\frac{1}{2}\) of 40 = 20 students.

→ (ii) Football = \(\frac{1}{4}\) of 40 = 10 students.

→ (iii) Badminton = Total - (Cricket + Football) = 40 - (20 + 10) = 40 - 30 = 10 students.

Marking: 1 mark per correct calculation.

Ref: NCERT Class 4 Mathematics, Ch. 12

SECTION D — 5 Marks

Q10.   Bar Graph & Data Interpretation   [5 Marks]

→ (a) Graphical Drawing: X-axis should show Days, Y-axis should show Number of Absentees. The height of the bars must accurately reflect: Mon=4, Tue=2, Wed=0, Thu=3, Fri=5. [3 Marks for drawing]

→ (b) Wednesday, because there were 0 absentees.

→ (c) Total absentees = 4 + 2 + 0 + 3 + 5 = 14 absentees.

Marking: part (a) 3 marks for neat graph with labelled axes and correct bar heights. Part (b) 1 mark for Wednesday. Part (c) 1 mark for total 14.
