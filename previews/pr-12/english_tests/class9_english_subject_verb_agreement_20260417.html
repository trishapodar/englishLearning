Q3.   is   [1 Mark(s)]

Accept: is
Common Error: Using 'are' treating it as two distinct items instead of a single compound concept.

Q4.   was   [1 Mark(s)]

was

SECTION B — 2 Mark(s) Each

Q5.   Error: lend -> Correct: lends   [2 Mark(s)]

Error: lend. Correct Sentence: A bouquet of yellow roses lends colour and fragrance to the room.

Marking: 1 mark for identifying the error ('lend'). 1 mark for the correct verb ('lends').
Accept: lends
Common Error: Failing to recognize 'bouquet' is the singular subject, not the plural 'roses'.

Ref: NCERT Class 9 English Grammar

Q6.   Error: are -> Correct: is   [2 Mark(s)]

Error: are. Correct Sentence: The furniture in these rooms is very expensive.

Marking: 1 mark for identifying the error ('are'). 1 mark for the correct verb ('is').
Accept: is
Common Error: Assuming 'furniture' is plural or getting confused by 'rooms'.

Ref: NCERT Class 9 English Grammar
